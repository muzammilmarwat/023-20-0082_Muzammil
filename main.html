The Flying Cat

The Cat Who Could Fly

Jerome the cat could fly. He loved to soar through the air, over the trees and houses. One day, he saw a little girl lost in the forest. He helped her find her way home and decided to become a superhero. He called himself "The Cat Who Could Fly" and saved people from danger.

Jerome was a kind and brave cat. He always used his powers to help others. He was a true hero.
